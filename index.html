About the Team:

After the obliteration of Pallet Town at the hands of the nefarious Pokemon trainer only known as "Moz the Destroyer," the once peaceful world of Pokemon was left reeling from the ruthlessness, insatiability, sheer pervertedness of Moz's tyranny. Having failed to stop Moz's assault on Pallet Town, the illustrious trainers Ronnie Digital and rogue known as l33t soon began to question the world around them and the true nature of the Pokemon creatures. With the betrayal of their closest ally to Moz, Lady Cataclysm, still burning in their hearts, the once celebrated heroes fled from society into reclusivity.

Despondent and nihilistic, the two wandered the Johto desert for 2 years, subsisting solely on defeated Fearows and Sandshrews. It was during this time that the two came to the belief that Pokemon were nothing more than tools for destruction, a Darwinian testament to the capriciousness and deterministic nature of the PokeUniverse.
