About the Team:

After the obliteration of Pallet Town at the hands of the nefarious Pokémon trainer known as "Moz the Destroyer," the once peaceful Pokémon World was left reeling. The fall of SEI5's last defenses against Moz at Pallet Town ushered in a new era of ruthless instability and sadistic perversion under Moz's tyrannical rule. Narrowly escaping Moz's final attack, the elite trainers Ronnie Digital and l33t retreated to the Johto desert. With the betrayal of their closest ally, Lady Cataclysm, to Moz's forces still stinging in their hearts, the once celebrated heroes fled into obscurity, ultimately questioning the true nature of the world around them and the mysterious beings known as Pokémon.

Despondent and nihilistic, the two wandered the Johto desert for 2 years surviving on slain Fearows and Sandshrews. The harsh and unforgiving environment and inherent savagery of Pokémon in the wild taught them much: this world had no rule. Pokémon themselves were nothing if not the embodiment of an uncaring God, a Darwinian testament to the capricious, deterministic nature of the PokéVerse and the little creatures that inhabit it.

With their newfound philosophy, l33t and Ronnie have begun their travel around the world in search of the strongest and rarest Pokémon--attaining them through any means necessary. Forced into disguise due to the ubiquity of Moz's agents of evil, they've reassembled themselves under the new banner of Team Rocket. The war will be long and arduous. l33t and Ronnie are currently looking for recruits in only the most talented Pokemon trainers with hopes of not only dethroning the diabolical Moz, but bringing forth a new era of PokéKind based upon their newfound philosophy.
