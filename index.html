About the Team:

After the obliteration of Pallet Town at the hands of the nefarious Pokemon trainer only known as "Moz the Destroyer," the once peaceful world of Pokemon was left reeling from the ruthlessness, insatiability, and sheer pervertedness of Moz's tyranny. Having failed to stop Moz's assault on Pallet Town, the illustrious trainers Ronnie Digital and l33t soon began to question the world around them and the true nature Pokemon. With the betrayal of their closest ally, Lady Cataclysm, to Moz still burning in their hearts, the once celebrated heroes fled into reclusivity.

Despondent and nihilistic, the two wandered the Johto desert for 2 years observing the inherent savagery of Pokemon in the wild. It was during this time that the two came to the belief that Pokemon were nothing if not the embodiment of an uncaring God, a Darwinian testament to the capriciousness and deterministic nature of the PokeUniverse and all those who inhabit it.

With their newfound philosophy, l33t and Ronnie are currently traveling the world in search of the strongest and rarest Pokemon through any means necessary. Under the new banner of Team Rocket, l33t and Ronnie are assembling an army of only the most talented Pokemon trainers in hopes of not only dethroaning the diabolical Moz, but bringing forth a new era of PokeKind.
